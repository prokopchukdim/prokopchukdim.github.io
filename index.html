About Me

Hi, I'm Dmitriy, a Software Engineer studying at the University of Toronto.

I'm currently a Software Engineer Co-op at PointClickCare, where I work on cloud-native microservices using Java Springboot, React, and MS SQL Server.

I was recently a Research Assistant at the Ontario Institute for Studies in Education, where I used Python, Selenium, and BeautifulSoup to develop 20+ web-scraping and database formatting tools to advance research on university sustainability metrics.

I am also a recipient of the 2021 Lester B. Pearson Scholarship, a full-ride four-year scholarship, thanks to my consistent record of academic success, achievement, and breadth of interest.

While I am most comfortable delivering solutions in Java, Python, C, and SQL, I'm always trying to learn something new, whether that be a new technology, framework, or hobby.
